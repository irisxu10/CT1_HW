P5.js Time and Oscillation

Week5 Assignment and Process of my work

In this week's assignment, I created an interactive project using P5.js. It includes a pendulum-like ball, with one end of the rod fixed at the center of the canvas and the other attached to the ball. The ball rotates at a constant speed around the center. Additionally, there is a square that rotates 15 degrees clockwise and changes color randomly every time the ball passes its initial position three times. I also used a time function to generate a small square on the canvas at regular intervals.

Step2

In the third step, I had the thread attached to the ball around the center point of the picture.

Step4

Next, I placed the square in the center. Ready to make the connection between the square and the rotation of the ball. I was going to have the square turn 15 degrees clockwise for every 3 revolution of the ball.

Step5

Then I generate a square of random size and color at a random location every time the square rotates.
