Week14 Multi-channel music controller

In this project I used the ESP32 and three sensors to control sound synthesis and playback in Processing. Knobs are used to select notes, buttons trigger sounds, and an infrared sensor controls the wet of the sound effect.

Process

First I chose three sensors and connected them to the breadboard to test if the three sensors could WORK (by the change in value)

Next I started to place the bread version in the box I had prepared with the SENSOR exposed at the top of the box.
